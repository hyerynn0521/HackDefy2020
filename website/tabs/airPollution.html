What is Air Pollution?

Air pollution is caused by harmful or high quantities of substances
that are introduced into Earth's atmosphere. This is caused by
extensive human activity such as gases emitted from factories and cars.
These gases can destroy the ozone layer, which protects us from the
rays that can be harmful for the human body.

Some ways you can Help:

  • Eliminate the use of fireplace and woodstove. These produce gases that are harmful to the environment.

  • Use lightbulbs that are energy efficient, such as LED.

  • Try growing a plant or tree in your balcony or house! Plants produce clean air such as oxygen and help reduce air pollution.
